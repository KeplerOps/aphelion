# ADR-009: Defer the Replication and HA Mechanism Choice

- **Status**: Accepted
- **Date**: 2026-04-05
- **Superseded by**: None

## Context

ADR-003 commits to single-node first. ADR-005 keeps storage and transactions single-node. A concrete replication choice would be premature.

## Decision

Defer concrete replication and HA mechanism. Architecture must preserve boundaries for later work. No built-in HA claimed. No mechanism selected (log shipping, consensus, shared-storage failover, multi-primary). Selection is a later ADR.

## Consequences

**Positive:**
- Prevents speculative decisions about mechanisms the team hasn't validated.
- Keeps the architecture honest about what it does and does not provide.

**Negative:**
- HA-requiring buyers are out of scope for initial releases.

**Risks:**
- Unconscious assumptions about future HA shape creep into current code.
- Pressure to promise HA early leads to informal commitments.

1. Add `# adr-override: ADR-009` with a rationale comment in the code.
2. Document the deviation and rationale in the PR description.
3. Automated hooks will allow the override to pass.
