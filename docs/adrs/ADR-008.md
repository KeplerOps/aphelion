# ADR-008: Defer the Public Wire Protocol Choice

- **Status**: Accepted
- **Date**: 2026-04-05
- **Superseded by**: None

## Context

Auth, session lifecycle, observability, extension exposure, and upgrade contracts are not concrete enough for a durable protocol choice.

## Decision

Do not commit to a public wire protocol yet. No Bolt, PostgreSQL, gRPC, or HTTP-only commitment. Internal service interfaces are acceptable. Selection deferred until server contract and compatibility policy are concrete.

## Consequences

**Positive:**
- Avoids hardening the wrong protocol surface.
- Keeps options open for the right protocol choice once requirements are clear.

**Negative:**
- Early integration needs a temporary internal interface.
- Cannot claim ecosystem compatibility expectations (e.g., Bolt driver support).

**Risks:**
- Deferral becomes indefinite indecision.
- Internal APIs calcify into de facto public contracts.
